Adenocarcinoma of unknown primary may also be referred to as carcinoma of unknown primary site, cancer/carcinoma of unknown primary (CUP), occult primary, or unknown primary carcinoma.

|-

====Chemotherapy====
*[[Carboplatin (Paraplatin)]] AUC 6 IV over 30 minutes once on day 1, '''given first'''
*[[Paclitaxel (Taxol)]] 200 mg/m<sup>2</sup> IV over 3 hours once on day 1, '''given second'''

====Supportive medications====
*[[Methylprednisolone (Solumedrol)]] 32 mg PO 24 and 12 hours before paclitaxel, and 10 minutes before carboplatin

*[[Ranitidine (Zantac)]] 100 mg IV over 10 minutes prior to chemotherapy
*[[Ondansetron (Zofran)]] 8 mg IV after the above premedications
*Suggested: [[Filgrastim (Neupogen)]] 300 mcg SC once per day on days 5 to 12

'''21-day cycle for 6 to 8 cycles'''

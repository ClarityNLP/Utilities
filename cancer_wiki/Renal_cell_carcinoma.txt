====Chemotherapy====
*[[Axitinib (Inlyta)]] as follows:
**5 mg PO twice per day for at least 2 weeks
**Then if tolerated and BP not greater than 150/90, increased to 7 mg PO twice per day
**Then if tolerated and BP not greater than 150/90, increased to 10 mg PO twice per day
**Dose can be reduced to 2 to 3 mg PO twice per day if needed based on tolerability
